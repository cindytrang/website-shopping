Frequently Asked Questions

+
Who are we?
We are E-Veg, a dedicated online platform providing you with the freshest and highest-quality vegetables. Our team is passionate about delivering farm-fresh produce directly to your doorstep.
+
What payment methods do you accept?
We accept credit/debit cards and secure online payment gateways.
+
How can I track my order?
Once your order is dispatched, you will receive a confirmation email with a tracking link.
+
Where do our vegetables come from?
At E-veg our vegetables are sourced from local farmers and trusted suppliers who share our commitment to quality and sustainability. We prioritize freshness, ensuring that you receive the best produce available.
+
How can I place an order?
Placing an order with us is easy! Simply browse our selection, add your desired vegetables to your cart, and proceed to checkout. Follow the steps to provide delivery details and complete your purchase.
+
When will I receive my order?
Delivery times vary based on your location and selected delivery slot. You can choose a convenient time during the checkout process, and we will make every effort to deliver your order within the specified time frame.
+
Do you offer returns or refunds?
Yes, we have a hassle-free return policy. If you are not satisfied with your purchase, please contact us within 30 days, and we will arrange for a return and refund.
